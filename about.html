About This Game

Leadership: The Moral Simulator is a strategic decision-making game designed to improve critical thinking, ethical reasoning, and leadership skills.

Across 100 structured levels, players face real-world inspired scenarios involving resource management, moral dilemmas, negotiation, strategy, and team dynamics.

The goal of this game is not just to win, but to think. Every decision reflects your judgment, patience, and values.

This project was independently designed and developed to promote analytical thinking and structured problem solving.
